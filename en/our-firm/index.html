R&H Legal Services is a private Colombian law firm specialized in supporting individuals, families, foreign companies and investors in their legal processes in Colombia.

Our Story

Founded to serve those who choose Colombia

R&H Legal Services was born from the conviction that foreigners who decide to live, work, or invest in Colombia deserve top-tier legal advice: clear, ethical, and efficient.

For years, our founder Ximena Holguín Reyes identified an important gap in the market: international clients navigating the Colombian legal system without adequate guidance, facing rejections, fines, and avoidable delays. The firm was born out of that need.

Today, R&H Legal Services operates as a high-performance boutique firm, serving clients in over 20 countries with a bilingual, personalized approach focused on concrete, measurable results.

Mission & Values

What defines us as a firm

Mission

To provide specialized, transparent, and effective legal advice to individuals and foreign companies interacting with the Colombian legal system, ensuring reliable results and long-term relationships.

Vision

To be the reference firm in Colombia for international clients requiring legal services in immigration law, intellectual property, and corporate law.

Professional Ethics

Efficiency

Lasting Relationship
