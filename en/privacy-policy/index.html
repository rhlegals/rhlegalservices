Privacy Policy & Personal Data Processing

Last updated: January 2025

1. Data Controller

RH Legal Services (hereinafter "RH Legal" or "the Firm"), domiciled in Colombia, is responsible for the processing of the personal data you provide through this website or any other means of communication.

Controller contact: info@rhlegales.com

2. Legal Framework

This policy is governed by Statutory Law 1581 of 2012 (Colombia's Personal Data Protection Act), Regulatory Decree 1377 of 2013, and other applicable concordant and complementary regulations.

3. Personal Data We Collect

Through contact forms and other means, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone or mobile number
  • Information about the service of interest
  • Data you voluntarily share in communications

4. Purposes of Processing

Your personal data will be used to:

  • Respond to your legal advisory requests and inquiries.
  • Provide contracted or prospective legal services.
  • Send relevant information about our services (with your consent).
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations.
  • Improve the quality of our services and communications.

5. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under Law 1581 of 2012, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Update and correct your data when inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Erasure of your data when no longer needed for the declared purpose.
  • Revoke the authorization granted for processing your data.
  • Access your data free of charge at least once per month.
  • File complaints with the Superintendence of Industry and Commerce (SIC) for violations of applicable regulations.

To exercise your rights, send a request to: info@rhlegales.com

6. Data Security

RH Legal adopts reasonable technical, human, and administrative measures to ensure the security of your personal data, preventing unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or improper disclosure.

7. Transfer of Data to Third Parties

RH Legal does not sell, rent, or transfer your personal data to third parties without your consent, except where necessary for the delivery of contracted services (e.g., immigration authorities, SIC) or as required by law.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

This website may use cookies and similar technologies (such as Google Analytics / Google Tag Manager) to analyze traffic and improve user experience. You may configure your browser to reject cookies; however, some features may be affected.

9. Policy Validity

This policy is effective from the date shown in its header. RH Legal reserves the right to modify it at any time. Material changes will be notified through this website.

10. Contact

For any questions related to this policy or the processing of your data, contact us at:

RH Legal Services

Email: info@rhlegales.com
